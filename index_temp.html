An Introduction to the Royal College History Club


The History Club of Royal College was established during the 1970s as the "Natural History Club" with the whole Art Section as its first members. The Natural History Club existed until the 1990s after it was disbanded due to a lack of members and inactivity. But in 2013 with the guidance of Mr. Palitha Abeywardena, the club was founded and reactivated. After enormous and successful projects, such as "Wahalkada '16" carried out by the past chairmen the History Club of Royal College has reached as A graded club.
